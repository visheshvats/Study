# 05. Prophylaxis and Planning

## Prophylaxis
Thinking about what your opponent wants to do, and stopping it.
- **Question**: "If it were my opponent's turn, what would they play?"
- **Action**: Prevent their active plan *before* pursuing your own.
- **Famous Master**: Tigran Petrosian (The Iron Tigran).

## Creating a Plan
1.  Evaluate the position (See Lesson 01).
2.  Find a target (Weak pawn, exposed King).
3.  Formulate a plan to attack it.
    *   "I will double Rooks on the c-file."
    *   "I will push my h-pawn to open the King's position."

## Drills
1.  Play a game against a low-level engine (1000 Elo). Before EVERY move, say out loud what the engine threatens. If nothing, say "Nothing".
