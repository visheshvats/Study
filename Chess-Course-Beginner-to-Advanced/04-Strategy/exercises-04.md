# Module 04 Exercises

## Part A: Evaluation
1.  **Material**: You have R+B vs R+N. Is material equal?
2.  **Activity**: Your Rook is on a7 (7th rank). Opponent's Rook is on a1 (passive). Who has the advantage?
3.  **weakness**: You have "Doubled Isolated Pawns". Are these usually a strength or weakness?

## Part B: Concepts
4.  If you have less space (cramped), should you trade pieces? (Yes/No).
5.  What is a "Bad Bishop"?
6.  What is "Prophylaxis"?

## Part C: Planning
7.  **Scenario**: You have a Pawn chain pointing to the Kingside (e4, d5, c3 vs e6, d6). Where should you attack?
    a) Queenside
    b) Center
    c) Kingside

8.  **Scenario**: Your opponent has a lovely Knight on e5 that is killing you. You have a passive Bishop on c1.
    What is a good strategic goal?
    a) Push pawns randomly.
    b) Try to trade your Bad Bishop for his Good Knight.
    c) Resign.
