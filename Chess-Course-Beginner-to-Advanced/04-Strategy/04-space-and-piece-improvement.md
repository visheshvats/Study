# 04. Space and Piece Improvement

## Space advantage
- **Definition**: You control more squares behind your own pawns, giving your pieces room to move.
- **Rule**: If you have a space advantage, **do not trade pieces**. (Keep their pieces cramped).
- **Rule**: If you are cramped, **trade pieces** to make room.

## Piece Improvement
- **"The Worst Piece Rule"**: Find your worst piece. Improve it.
- **Maneuvering**: Sometimes you need 3 moves to get a Knight from a bad square to a good one. Do it.

## Drills
1.  Set up a LOCKED position (pawns blocking everything). Maneuver a Knight from h1 to a8. (Just to practice the vision of paths).
