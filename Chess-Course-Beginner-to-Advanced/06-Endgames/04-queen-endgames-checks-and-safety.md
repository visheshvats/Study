# 04. Queen Endgames: Checks and Safety

## The General Rule
- Queen endgames are Long, Hard, and checking.
- **Goal**: Create a Passed Pawn and escort it.
- **Centralization**: A Queen in the center is the best defense against perpetual check.

## Perpetual Check
- The losing side's best friend.
- If you are winning, do not expose your King.
- Hide your King behind your own pawns.
