# 03. Minor Piece Endgames

## Knight Endgames
- Knights are like pawns. They can't cross the board fast.
- **Factor**: Pawn structure on one side vs both sides.
- Knights favor *one side* of the board play.
- **Outside Passed Pawn**: Deadly against Knights (Knight has to run to catch it, can't fight in center).

## Bishop Endgames
- **Opposite Colored Bishops**: High drawing tendency (even down 2 pawns!). You block on your color; they can't touch you.
- **Same Colored Bishops**: The side with better pawns (on opposite color of bishop) wins.

## Drills
1.  Set up K+B+P vs K+B (Opposite colors). Try to win. (You probably can't if the defender is sane).
