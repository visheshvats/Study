# 02. Rook Endgames (Lucena & Philidor)

"All Rook endgames are drawn." - Tartakower (He was joking, but they are hard).

## 1. The Philidor Position (Defense)
**Goal**: Draw K+R+P vs K+R.
- **Setup**: Defending King on the promotion square (e.g., e8). Pawn on e5.
- **Method**: Put your Rook on the 6th rank (e.g., Ra6).
- The enemy King cannot advance past the 6th rank.
- When the Pawn pushes to e6, **IMMEDIATELY** move Rook to 1st rank (Ra1). Then give checks from behind.

## 2. The Lucena Position (Winning)
**Goal**: Win K+R+P vs K+R.
- **Setup**: Your Pawn is on the 7th rank. Your King is in front of it (e.g., e8). Enemy Rook cuts you off.
- **Method**: "Building a Bridge".
    1.  Push enemy King away (check).
    2.  Bring your Rook to the 4th rank.
    3.  Move your King out.
    4.  Block the checks with your Rook.

## Drills
1.  Memorize Philidor. If you know it, you save 50% of your lost games.
