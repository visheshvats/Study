# 01. King and Pawn Endgames: The Opposition

The King is now a fighting piece. Use it!

## The Opposition
When two Kings face each other with one square in between.
- **Rule**: Whoever has to move *loses* the opposition. (Ideally, you want *their* King to have to move, so you can invade).
- **Direct Opposition**: K on e4 vs K on e6. White to move? Black has the opposition.

## The Square of the Pawn
Can the King catch the pawn?
- Draw a diagonal line from the pawn to the promotion rank.
- If the King is inside that box, he catches it.

## Key Lucena Position (Pawn on 7th)
Wait, that's Rook endgames. For K+P:
- **Winning**: King is *in front* of the pawn and has opposition.
- **Drawing**: King is *behind* the pawn or enemy King controls the promotion square.
