# 05. Practical Endgame Technique

## How to Win a Won Game
1.  **Do not hurry**. (Unless it's a race).
2.  **Activate your King**. (In the ending, the King is a monster).
3.  **Schematic Thinking**. think in "pictures". "I want my Knight on e5 and my King on f6." Then calculate how to get there.

## Fortress
- Sometimes you are down material, but you can build a wall.
- Ex: Rook + Pawn vs Rook. If you are the defender, you can draw with Philidor.
