# Module 05 Exercises

## Part A: Structures
1.  **IQP**: You have an Isolated Queen Pawn on d4. Should you try to trade Queens? (Yes/No).
2.  **Minority Attack**: What are you trying to create?
    a) A checkmate attack.
    b) A weak enemy pawn (backward pawn).
    c) A passed pawn for yourself immediately.

## Part B: Assessment
3.  **Scenario**: You are White. You successfully launched a "Greek Gift" (Bxh7+).
    Black's King is running to f8.
    Should you:
    a) Sit back and think for 20 minutes calculating the kill.
    b) Play the first check you see.

4.  **Conversion**: You are up a Rook. Your opponent attacks your King.
    Should you:
    a) Try to mate him first.
    b) Sacrifice your Queen for his Queen to stop the attack and go to an endgame.

## Part C: Analysis
5.  Why is the pawn on c6 weak after a successful Minority Attack (b5xc6)?
    (Hint: Can another pawn protect it?)
