# 05. Transitioning Advantage Conversion

" The hardest game to win is a won game." - Lasker

## Simplification
- **Rule**: If you are **Up Material**, trade pieces (not pawns).
- **Why?** Being up a Knight is huge in an endgame. In a middlegame, they can still mate you.

## Transitioning to Endgame
- If you have an attack that stalled, don't force it.
- Look for a way to enter a favorable endgame.
- Ex: Trade Queens if it guarantees you entering a winning K+P ending.

## Drills
1.  Set up a position up a pieace (Knight). Play against Stockfish Level 8. Your ONLY goal is to trade pieces until you are in an endgame.
