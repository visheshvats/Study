# 02. Isolated Queen Pawn (IQP)

When you have a d-pawn and no c- or e-pawns.

## Playing WITH the IQP
- **Goal**: Attack!
- You have more space and active pieces. e5 and c5 squares are often outposts for Knights.
- **Plan**: Battery Rooks on d-file or e-file. Attack the King. Push `d4-d5` at the right moment.

## Playing AGAINST the IQP
- **Goal**: Blockade and Trade.
- **Weakness**: The d-pawn cannot be defended by other pawns. It is an endgame weakness.
- **Plan**: Put a Knight on d5 (Blockade). Trade Queens (kills the attack). Win the endgame.

## Drills
1.  Play a game starting `1. e4 e6 2. d4 d5 3. exd5 exd5 4. c4`. (Exchange French with c4). Getting an IQP.
