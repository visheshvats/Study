# 04. Minority Attack

Using fewer pawns to attack a larger pawn chain.
Target: Carlsbad Structure (e.g., QGD Exchange Variation).
- White: a2, b2. Black: a7, b7, c6, d5.
- White pushes **b4-b5**!

## Goal
To exchange b5 vs c6.
- This leaves Black with a **Weak Backward Pawn** on c6 or d5.
- It opens the c-file for White's Rooks.

## Plan
1.  Preparation: Rb1, a4, b4.
2.  Action: b5.
3.  Target: The resulting weakness.
