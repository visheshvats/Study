# 01. Attacking the King

## When to Attack?
- **Rule**: Attack when you have a advantage in the center, or the center is closed.
- **Rule**: Attack when the opponent's King lacks defenders.

## Common Patterns

### 1. Greek Gift Sacrifice (Bxh7+)
- White Bishop takes on h7 (checking).
- Knight jumps to g5.
- Queen comes to h5.
- **Result**: Usually mate or winning material.

### 2. The Pawn Storm (Opposite Castling)
- You castled Queenside. They castled Kingside.
- **Plan**: Push your g and h pawns to open lines!
- "Pawn Storm".

## Drills
1.  Study the "Greek Gift" pattern until you can spot it in 1 second.
