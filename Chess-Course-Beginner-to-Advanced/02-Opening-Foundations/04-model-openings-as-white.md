# 04. Model Openings as White

Recommendation: **The Italian Game**
Logical, principled, and leads to open games rich in tactics.

## The Setup
1.  **e4**: Controls center, opens Bishop/Queen.
2.  **Nf3**: Develops Knight, attacks e5.
3.  **Bc4**: The Italian Bishop. Attacks f7 (Black's weak spot) and controls center.
4.  **c3**: (Giuoco Piano) Preparing to play d4 and build a big pawn center.
5.  **d3**: (Giuoco Pianissimo) A quieter, solid approach.

## Key Plans
- Castle Kingside quickly.
- Play `h3` to prevent pins if needed.
- Maneuver the Knight: `Nbd2 -> f1 -> g3` (Typical maneuver).
- Eventually play `d4` to challenge the center.

## Example Line
1.  e4 e5
2.  Nf3 Nc6
3.  Bc4 Bc5
4.  c3 Nf6
5.  d3 d6
6.  O-O O-O

## Alternative: The London System (d4)
If you prefer solid, safe structures.
1.  d4, 2. Bf4, 3. e3, 4. Nf3, 5. c3, 6. Nbd2.
(The "Pyramid" pawn structure c3-d4-e3).

## Drills
1.  Play 5 games as White forcing the Italian Setup (e4, Nf3, Bc4).
