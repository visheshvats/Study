# 05. Model Openings as Black

## Against e4: The "Two Knights Defense" (solid)
1.  e4 e5
2.  Nf3 Nc6
3.  Bc4 **Nf6!**
    - You attack e4 immediately.
    - If White plays `4. Ng5` (Fried Liver attempt), play `4... d5`.
    - If White plays `4. d3`, just play `4... Bc5` and `5... d6`.

## Against d4: The Queen's Gambit Declined (QGD)
1.  d4 d5
2.  c4 e6!
    - Simple and solid. You support the d5 pawn.
    - Plan: Develop Knight to f6, Bishop to e7, Castle.
    - Challenge the center later with `...c5`.

## Principles When Surprised
What if they play `1. b3` or `1. f4`?
- **Don't Panic.**
- **Plan**: Put pawns in the center (d5/e5).
- Develop Knights to natural squares.
- Castle.
- Don't try to refute it immediately. Just play chess.

## Drills
1.  Play 3 games as Black. If they play e4, meet it with e5. If d4, meet it with d5.
