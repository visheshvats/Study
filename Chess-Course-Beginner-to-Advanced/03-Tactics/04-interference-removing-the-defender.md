# 04. Interference and Removing the Defender

## Interference
Blocking the line of defense.
- **Concept**: A Queen is protecting a square from far away. You shove a piece in the middle of that line. The connection is broken.
- **Tip**: Look for two pieces communicating over a long distance.

## Removing the Defender
Simplest tactic in the world.
1.  Identify the piece defending the target.
2.  Capture it, distract it, or chase it away.
3.  Capture the target.

## Drills
1.  Look for puzzles tagged "Interference". They are rare but satisfying.
2.  Always ask: "Who is defending that piece?" and "Can I kill him?"
