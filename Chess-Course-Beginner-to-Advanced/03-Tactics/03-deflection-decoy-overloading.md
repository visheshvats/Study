# 03. Deflection, Decoy, Overloading

## Deflection
Forcing a defender to *leave* the square it is guarding.
- Ex: The King is guarding the Queen. Check the King to force him away. Take the Queen.

## Decoy
Luring a piece *to* a bad square.
- Ex: Sacrificing a piece on h7 to lure the King out, then mating him.

## Overloading
A defensive piece has too many jobs.
- Ex: A Bishop is guarding a Knight AND a Rook.
- Capture the Knight. If Bishop takes back, take the Rook. The Bishop was "Overloaded".
- **Concept**: "The overworked piece."

## Drills
1.  Find a puzzle where a piece does 2 jobs. Remove one job (by sacrifice) and see what happens.
