# Module 03 Exercises

**Instructions**: Write answers down. Check solutions.

## Part A: Definitions
1.  What is the difference between a Pin and a Skewer?
2.  Which piece is the best at "Forking"?
3.  What does "Removing the Defender" achieve?

## Part B: Visualization (No Board)
4.  White Rook on a1, White Rook on h1. Black King on e8.
    White plays `Rh8+`. Is it checkmate? (Assume huge board, no other pieces).
5.  White Bishop on c1. Black Rook on h8.
    Can the Bishop ever capture the Rook? (What color is h8?)

## Part C: Tactics Identification
6.  **Scenario**: White Knight on f5. Black King on g8. Black Queen on d8.
    If White plays `Ne7+`, what is this tactic called?
    a) Pin
    b) Skewer
    c) Fork

7.  **Scenario**: White Queen on d1, White Bishop on g5. Black Knight on f6. Black Queen on d8.
    The Knight is pinned. If Black moves the Knight, he loses the Queen.
    What kind of pin is this? (Absolute or Relative?)

8.  **Calculation**:
    You see a move that wins a Rook. But it allows your opponent to checkmate you in 1 move.
    Should you play it? (Yes/No).
