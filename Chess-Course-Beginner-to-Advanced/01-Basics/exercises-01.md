# Module 01 Exercises

**Instructions**: Write your answers on a piece of paper, then check `solutions-01.md`.

## Part A: Notation & Board
1.  What color is the square **h1**?
2.  Write the move: Bishop captures Knight on f6 with check.
3.  Write the move: Queenside Castle.
4.  If a White Pawn is on e2, can it move to e4?

## Part B: Rules & Movement
5.  Can a King move to a square adjacent to the enemy King?
6.  Can a Knight jump over an enemy Queen?
7.  If a pawn reaches the end, can it become a King?
8.  Is En Passant mandatory?

## Part C: Tactics & Situations
9.  **White to Move. Is this checkmate?**
    White Rook on a8. Black King on h8. Black Pawns on h7, g7, f7.
    (Yes/No)

10. **Counting**:
    Black Knight on d5.
    White attacks it with: N, B, P.
    Black defends it with: P, Q.
    Can White win material on d5?

11. **Stalemate Check**:
    White: King on f7, Pawn on g7.
    Black: King on h8.
    White to move. If White plays `g8=Q`, is it checkmate or stalemate?
