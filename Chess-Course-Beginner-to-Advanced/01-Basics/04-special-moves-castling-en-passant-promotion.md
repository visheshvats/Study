# 04. Special Moves

## Castling (O-O or O-O-O)
The only move where two pieces move at once (King and Rook).
**Purpose**: Get King to safety, bring Rook to immediate action.
**How**:
1.  King moves **2 squares** towards the Rook.
2.  Rook jumps over the King to the adjacent square.

**Rules**:
- King and Rook must have **never moved**.
- Cannot castle **out of check**, **through check**, or **into check**.
- No pieces between them.

## En Passant
A special pawn capture.
- **Condition**: Enemy pawn moves **2 squares** forward from start and lands next to your pawn.
- **Action**: You can capture it *as if* it had moved only 1 square.
- **Timing**: Must be done **immediately** on the very next turn, or the right is lost forever.

## Promotion
When a pawn reaches the 8th rank (opposite side), it instantly transforms.
- usually becomes a **Queen** (99% of time).
- Can become R, B, N (Underpromotion).
- Cannot remain a pawn or become a King.

## Key Ideas
- Castle early! (Usually by move 10).
- Don't forget En Passant exists (it's often a tactical surprise).

## Drills
1.  Practice Kingside and Queenside castling physically.
2.  Set up an En Passant scenario to ensure you understand the mechanics.
