# 05. Preparation and Psychology

## Preparation
- **Opening Prep**: Don't just memorize. Understand the resulting middlegames.
- **Opponent Prep**: Look at their games. Do they play aggressive? Play solid. Do they play passive? Attack them.

## Psychology
- **Fear**: Don't fear the rating. Fear the moves.
- **Confidence**: Believe in your calculation.
- **Tilt**: If you lose a game, STOP. Take a walk. Do not "Revenge Queue".
