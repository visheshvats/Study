# 04. Defensive Technique and Resourcefulness

Defense is harder than attack. You have to be precise.

## Resourcefulness
- **Rule**: Never resign early.
- **Stalemate Tricks**: Look for them.
- **Perpetual Check**: The defender's salvation.

## Prophylactic Defense
- Don't wait until you are mated.
- If you see an attack coming, move your King away or build a fortress *now*.

## Drills
1.  Play "Bad Positions". Set up a losing position (down a pawn) against an engine. Try to survive for 50 moves.
