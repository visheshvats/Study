# 03. Complex Calculation and Tree Pruning

At a high level, you can't calculate everything. You must "prune" the tree.

## Tree Pruning
- **Scenario**: You have 5 candidate moves.
- **Pruning**: Quickly reject 3 of them based on intuition or quick shallow check ("No, that loses a piece").
- **Deep Dive**: Spend your remaining time only on the 2 plausible ones.

## Visualization Techniques
- **"Stepping Stones"**: Visualize the position after 3 moves. Freeze it in your mind. Treat that as the new starting position. Then calculate 3 more.
- **Verification**: Before you move, assume your calculation was wrong. Look for the "Refutation" (The holes).
