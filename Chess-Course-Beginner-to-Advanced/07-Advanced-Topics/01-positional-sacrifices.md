# 01. Positional Sacrifices

Sacrificing material without a forced mate or immediate recovery.
**Goal**: To improve your position or ruin the opponent's.

## The Exchange Sacrifice (Rook for Knight/Bishop)
- **Classic**: Tigran Petrosian.
- **Why?**
    1.  To destroy a blockade.
    2.  To ruin pawn structure.
    3.  To dominate one color complex (Light/Dark squares).

## The Pawn Sack
- **Why?**
    1.  To open lines.
    2.  To gain tempo (Initiative).
    3.  To create an outpost.
