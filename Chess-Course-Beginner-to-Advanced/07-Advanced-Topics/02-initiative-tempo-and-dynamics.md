# 02. Initiative, Tempo, and Dynamics

## The Initiative
The ability to dictate the course of the game.
- "I threaten you, you respond."
- **Value**: The initiative is worth material. Often a Pawn or even a piece.
- **Rule**: If you have the initiative, **attack**. If you stop attacking, the initiative disappears, and now you are just down material.

## Dynamics
Chess is not static.
- A weakness is only a weakness if it can be exploited *in time*.
- **Dynamic Equality**: You have bad structure, but your pieces are flying around like crazy. It balances out.

## Drills
1.  Play the "Gambit" style. Play the King's Gambit or Evans Gambit in blitz to feel what it's like to have initiative for a pawn.
