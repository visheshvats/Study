# Assessment Answers

## Beginner
1.  Capture, Protect, Run.
2.  Control Center, Develop Pieces, King Safety.
3.  Light.
4.  Ra8# (Back Rank Mate). (Wait, R on a8? If no pieces between, and K on h8 trapped... Yes).
5.  5 points.

## Intermediate
1.  Bxh7+ sacrifice to expose the King.
2.  d-pawn with no neighbors. Strength (Space/Attack) & Weakness (Endgame).
3.  Pin, Fork, Skewer, Discovery.
4.  Opposition.
5.  Mate on h7 (Qxh7#).

## Advanced
1.  Rook ending, pawn on 7th, King in front. "Build a Bridge" with the Rook on 4th rank.
2.  Pushing a/b pawns to weaken c6. Used in Carlsbad/QGD exchange structures.
3.  It depends, but often it's a Fortress (Draw).
4.  Stopping opponent's plans before they happen.
5.  Eliminating bad candidate moves early to focus depth on good ones.
