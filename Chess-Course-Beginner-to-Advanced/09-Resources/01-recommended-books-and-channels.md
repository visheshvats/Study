# 01. Recommended Books and Channels

## YouTube Channels (Free)
1.  **Saint Louis Chess Club**: Master classes. Look for Yasser Seirawan's lectures.
2.  **Daniel Naroditsky**: "Speedrun" series (Explains thoughts while playing).
3.  **John Bartholomew**: "Chess Fundamentals" series (Undefended pieces).
4.  **GothamChess**: Entertaining recaps and "Guess the Elo".

## Books (Classic)
1.  **"Logical Chess: Move by Move"** by Irving Chernev (Best First Book).
2.  **"My System"** by Nimzowitsch (Classic strategy, hard read).
3.  **"100 Endgames You Must Know"** by Jesus de la Villa.

## Tools
1.  **Lichess.org**: Free (Tactics, Play, Analysis).
2.  **Chessable**: Spaced repetition for openings.
