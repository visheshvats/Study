# 03. Training Checklists

## Before a Tournament
- [ ] Solved 50 tactics daily for 1 week?
- [ ] Reviewed your Opening Repertoire lines?
- [ ] Slept 8 hours?

## Daily Check
- [ ] Did I blunder a piece today?
- [ ] Did I analyze my lost game?
- [ ] Did I learn one new thing?

## The "Tilt" Protocol
If you lose 3 games in a row:
1.  Stand up.
2.   Drink water.
3.  Stop playing for at least 1 hour.
