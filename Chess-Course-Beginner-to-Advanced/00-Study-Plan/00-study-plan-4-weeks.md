# 4-Week Study Plan

This plan assumes ~1 hour of study per day. If you have less time, extend the duration.

## Week 1: The Foundations (Modules 01 & 02)

| Day | Topic | Activity |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Mon** | Board & Moves | Read `01-Basics/01` & `02`. Practice moving pieces physically or on a screen. |
| **Tue** | Rules & Notation | Read `01-Basics/03` & `04`. Learn to read/write algebraic notation. |
| **Wed** | Basic Tactics | Read `01-Basics/05`. Solve `01-Basics/exercises-01.md`. |
| **Thu** | Opening Principles | Read `02-Opening-Foundations/01`. Play 2 games focusing on Center & Development. |
| **Fri** | Repertoire Start | Read `02-Opening-Foundations/02`, `04`, `05`. Pick one opening for White/Black. |
| **Sat** | Review & Play | Play 3 rapid games (10+5). Review using `00-Study-Plan/02-how-to-review-games.md`. |
| **Sun** | Rest / Light | Watch a recommended chess video (see Resources). |

## Week 2: Tactics & Calculation (Module 03)

| Day | Topic | Activity |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Mon** | Forks, Pins, Skewers | Read `03-Tactics/01`. Solve 10 theme puzzles. |
| **Tue** | Discovered Attacks | Read `03-Tactics/02`. Solve 10 theme puzzles. |
| **Wed** | Deflection & Interference | Read `03-Tactics/03` & `04`. Solve 10 theme puzzles. |
| **Thu** | Calculation | Read `03-Tactics/05`. Do one deep calculation exercise (10 min think time). |
| **Fri** | Assessment | Complete `03-Tactics/exercises-03.md`. |
| **Sat** | Play & Analyze | Play 3 rapid games. Focus on *not hanging pieces*. |
| **Sun** | Rest / Light | Solve simple mate-in-1 puzzles for speed. |

## Week 3: Strategy & Planning (Modules 04 & 05)

| Day | Topic | Activity |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Mon** | Evaluation & Pawns | Read `04-Strategy/01` & `02`. Assess 3 random positions. |
| **Tue** | Pieces & Planning | Read `04-Strategy/03` & `04`. |
| **Wed** | Middlegame Plans | Read `05-Middlegame-Planning/01` & `02`. Study the "Isolated Queen Pawn". |
| **Thu** | Structures | Read `05-Middlegame-Planning/03` & `04`. |
| **Fri** | Strategy Exercises | Complete `04-Strategy/exercises-04.md` and `05-Middlegame-Planning/exercises-05.md`. |
| **Sat** | Play & Analyze | Play 3 rapid games. Try to form a *plan* after the opening. |
| **Sun** | Rest / Light | Review one master game (e.g., Capablanca or Morphy). |

## Week 4: Endgames & Advanced Concepts (Modules 06 & 07)

| Day | Topic | Activity |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Mon** | King & Pawn | Read `06-Endgames/01`. Practice opposition against an engine. |
| **Tue** | Rook Endgames | Read `06-Endgames/02`. Memorize Lucena & Philidor positions. |
| **Wed** | Advanced Concepts | Read `07-Advanced-Topics/01` & `02`. |
| **Thu** | Defense & Psych | Read `07-Advanced-Topics/04` & `05`. |
| **Fri** | Endgame Drills | Complete `06-Endgames/exercises-06.md`. |
| **Sat** | Final Assessment | complete `08-Assessment/01-skill-check-beginner.md` (and others if ready). |
| **Sun** | Graduation | Review your progress. Set goals for the next month. |
